Desserts

Cupcakes

Cupcakes are a individual sized cake usually topped with frosting.

Cakes

Cakes are a dessert that is usually covered in frosting and topped with decorations.

Macaroons

Macaroons are wafers with a cream filling. Sometimes, they are filled with fruit jellys as well.

Ice Cream Sundaes

Ice Cream Sundaes consist of ice cream and toppings like fudge, cherries or nuts.

Brownies

Brownies are a chocolate delicacy that can be served with fudge or ice cream.

Pies

Pie is a fruity dessert that is has a crust on top. Pie is usually topped with ice cream.

Tarts

Tarts are similar to pies, but they do not have a top. Tarts are usually topped with fruit.

Cheesecake

Cheesecake is a desert that is made with cream cheese with a crust. It is usually topped with fruit or caramel.

Cookie

Cookies are simple deserts that come in different variations. The most common cookie is a chocolate chip cookie.
